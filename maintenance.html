Maintenance Services
Everything you need to keep your fire safety systems ready — and compliant with BFP requirements — all year long.

Extinguisher Refilling & Recharging

Professional refilling and recharging of all extinguisher types after discharge or expiry of agent charge. All refills include a hydrostatic test tag.

  • Dry powder (ABC), CO₂, wet chemical, foam
  • All cylinder sizes from 1 kg to 50 kg
  • Pressure test & certification tag
  • Walk-in or pickup-delivery service

Annual System Servicing

Scheduled annual maintenance visit covering inspection, testing, minor adjustments, and lubrication of all fire protection system components.

  • FDAS panel & battery service
  • Valve exercise & lubrication
  • Sprinkler head condition check
  • Written service report issued

Safety & Compliance Reports

Comprehensive maintenance logs and safety reports documenting all work performed — accepted by BFP for permit renewal and occupancy compliance.

  • Signed maintenance log book
  • Deficiency tracking & resolution
  • BFP-acceptable documentation
  • Digital records available on request

Corrective Repairs

Prompt repair or replacement of defective components identified during inspection or reported by building staff — prioritized by safety risk.

  • Detector & sounder replacement
  • Valve and fitting repairs
  • Control panel troubleshooting
  • Sprinkler head replacement

Spare Parts Supply

Stocked inventory of common replacement parts to minimize downtime — detectors, valve components, sprinkler heads, and alarm accessories.

  • OEM-compatible parts only
  • Same-day availability for common items
  • Sourcing service for special orders
  • Installed or supplied only

Emergency Response Service

Rapid response for urgent system failures — partial or full system outages, accidental discharge cleanup, and post-incident restoration.

  • Priority scheduling for emergencies
  • Accidental discharge cleanup
  • System isolation & restoration
  • Temporary measures while parts arrive

Extinguisher Types We Refill

  • DCP
    Dry Chemical Powder (ABC) Most common type — suitable for Class A, B, and C fires
  • CO₂
    Carbon Dioxide Ideal for electrical equipment and enclosed spaces, leaves no residue
  • WC
    Wet Chemical Specially formulated for commercial cooking fat fires (Class F/K)
  • AF
    AFFF Foam Effective against flammable liquid spills and Class B fires
  • Clean
    Clean Agent (Halotron / FM200) Non-damaging agent for sensitive electronics and archives
