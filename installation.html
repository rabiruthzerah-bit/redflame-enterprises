Installation Process

Five Stages to Completion

Every installation follows a disciplined five-stage process to ensure quality and code compliance.

01

Pre-Installation Survey

Site walkthrough to verify design drawings against actual conditions and coordinate with other trades.

02

Rough-In Work

Conduit, pipe runs, and brackets installed ahead of ceiling closure, coordinated with MEP works.

03

Equipment Installation

Panels, detectors, sprinkler heads, and all terminal devices fitted per approved drawings.

04

Testing & Commissioning

Pressure tests, functional tests, and system acceptance tests with full documentation.

05

Handover & As-Builts

As-built drawings, O&M manuals, and warranty documents issued at final handover.
