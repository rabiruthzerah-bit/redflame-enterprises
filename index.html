About Redflame

Redflame Enterprises is a prominent, licensed fire protection company and fire extinguisher manufacturer operating in Naga City, Camarines Sur. Established in 2009, we specialize in supplying certified fire safety equipment and engineering installation solutions across the Bicol Region.

We offer brand-new units and refilling services for all types of fire extinguishers, fire hose cabinets, emergency lights, smoke alarms, and sprinkler heads. Our technical services cover FDAS, automatic sprinkler, kitchen suppression, and FM200 systems.
