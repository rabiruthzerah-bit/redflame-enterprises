Maintenance Services

Keep Your Systems Ready

Scheduled and on-call maintenance for all fire protection equipment — so your systems work when they're needed most.

Extinguisher Refilling & Servicing

Professional refilling, recharging, and hydrostatic testing of all extinguisher types with certification tags and service records.

  • Dry powder, CO₂, wet chemical & foam
  • Pressure test & valve inspection
  • BFP certification tag issued

FDAS Preventive Maintenance

Periodic inspection and cleaning of all FDAS components to prevent false alarms and ensure detection reliability.

  • Detector cleaning & sensitivity test
  • Battery and backup power test
  • Panel log review & reset

Sprinkler System Maintenance

Annual inspection and preventive maintenance of sprinkler systems including valve testing and pipe condition checks.

  • Sprinkler head condition inspection
  • Valve exercise & lubrication
  • Pump run test & pressure check

Emergency Light Servicing

Battery replacement, bulb/LED check, and full duration testing of all emergency lights and exit signs.

  • Battery capacity test
  • Lamp replacement as needed
  • 90-minute discharge test

Kitchen Suppression Servicing

Semi-annual inspection and chemical agent replacement for kitchen suppression systems per NFPA 17A requirements.

  • Nozzle inspection & cleaning
  • Agent cylinder weighing
  • Fusible link replacement

Annual Safety Reports

Comprehensive written safety reports covering all fire protection systems — suitable for BFP renewal and insurance documentation.

  • System status summary
  • Deficiencies & recommendations
  • Service history records
