Our Design Process
A structured four-step approach from first site visit to permit-ready documents.
01

Site Assessment

We visit your building to evaluate occupancy type, hazard classification, existing systems, and all applicable code requirements.

02

System Engineering

Our engineers perform hydraulic calculations, detector placement analysis, and equipment selection — tailored to your layout.

03

Documentation

Full drawing set: floor plans, isometrics, schematics, equipment schedules, and technical specifications ready for submission.

04

Regulatory Submission

We assist with BFP and LGU submission, addressing review comments until design approval is secured.
