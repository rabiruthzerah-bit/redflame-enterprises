Testing Services

Full-System Verification

Every system we test is documented with a full report suitable for BFP inspection and building permit compliance.

Hydrostatic Pressure Testing

Pressure tests on sprinkler and standpipe systems to verify pipe integrity and detect leaks before commissioning.

  • 200 psi test pressure for 2 hours
  • Visual inspection of all joints
  • Written test certification

Alarm System Testing

Functional testing of all FDAS components — detectors, manual call points, panels, notification appliances, and suppression interfaces.

  • Detector sensitivity testing
  • Zone isolation & restore
  • Alarm output verification

Sprinkler System Inspection

Visual inspection and flow testing of automatic sprinkler systems to confirm code compliance and operational readiness.

  • Sprinkler head condition check
  • Main drain & inspector test
  • Valve position verification

Emergency Lighting Test

Duration and illumination testing of all emergency lights and exit signs to confirm adequate backup capacity.

  • Battery discharge test (90-min)
  • Lux level measurement
  • Faulty unit identification

Extinguisher Inspection

Visual and operational inspection of portable fire extinguishers — condition, pressure, pin and seal, and serviceability.

  • Pressure gauge check
  • Physical condition inspection
  • Tag and certification update

BFP Acceptance Testing

Coordinated acceptance testing performed in the presence of BFP inspectors for certificate of occupancy requirements.

  • Pre-inspection preparation
  • Live demonstration of all systems
  • Deficiency correction support
