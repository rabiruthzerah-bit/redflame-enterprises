Installation Process
Every installation follows a disciplined five-stage process to ensure quality and code compliance.
01

Pre-Installation Survey

Site walkthrough to verify design drawings against actual site conditions and coordinate with other trades.

02

Rough-In Work

Conduit, pipe runs, cable trays, and brackets installed ahead of ceiling closure — coordinated with civil and MEP works.

03

Equipment Installation

Panels, detectors, sprinkler heads, nozzles, and all terminal devices fitted and connected per approved drawings.

04

Testing & Commissioning

Pressure tests, functional tests, and system acceptance tests performed with full documentation of results.

05

Handover & As-Builts

As-built drawings, O&M manuals, and warranty documents issued to the owner at final handover.
