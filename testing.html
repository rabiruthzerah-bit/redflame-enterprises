Testing Services
We conduct all required acceptance tests and periodic inspections, with full written reports issued after each visit.

Hydrostatic Pressure Testing

Pressurize sprinkler and standpipe piping systems to verify structural integrity and confirm there are no leaks in fittings or pipe joints.

  • 200 psi (13.8 bar) test pressure
  • 2-hour sustained duration
  • Full leakage survey with documentation

Alarm System Functional Testing

End-to-end functional verification of FDAS panels, detectors, manual call points, and notification devices — zone by zone.

  • Detector sensitivity & response verification
  • Manual call point & abort station tests
  • Panel fault & trouble signal tests

Sprinkler Flow & Valve Tests

Inspector's test valve and main drain flow tests to confirm system pressure, water supply adequacy, and alarm activation.

  • Inspector's test valve discharge
  • Water flow alarm activation check
  • Control valve tamper switch test

Visual Inspection

Systematic visual survey of all accessible components to identify physical damage, obstructions, and non-conformances against the approved design.

  • Sprinkler head condition & clearance
  • Pipe hangers, supports, & labels
  • Extinguisher tags & accessibility

Extinguisher Inspection

Annual and monthly extinguisher inspections verifying charge, pressure, pin integrity, and condition — per BFP requirements.

  • Pressure gauge / weight check
  • Pin, seal, & label verification
  • Placement & signage check

Compliance Certification

Issue test reports and compliance certificates required for building permits, BFP inspections, and occupancy renewals.

  • Signed test report with findings
  • Deficiency list & corrective actions
  • Certificate of system compliance
